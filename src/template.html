Battleship

About Game

Battleship is a strategy board game where players take turns trying to sink each other's ships. It's also known as Battleships or Sea Battle.

How to play

  • Players place their fleet of five ships on a grid-like board.
  • Players take turns calling out shots at the other player's ships.
  • Players mark hits with red pegs.
  • Players continue to take turns until they've sunk all of their opponent's ships.
